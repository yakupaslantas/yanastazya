astazya 1:1

Mine assay of affection with her did conclude, her diligence lacking, wherefore she did betray me. Swift was her repentance, yet I did bid her cease and begone. And lo, she did obey, ne'er to return, until tidings I whispered to her companions. Thenceforth, she came to render judgment upon me, and all did languish in a torpor, until the day she unearthed the privy discourse shared 'twixt us.


astazya 1:2

Hark, the woman, now ensnared by life's swift currents, doth act with scant heed. Though warned oft, she turned to dark witchcraft, and thus, ill fates did befall her.
